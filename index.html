Food is any substance an organism consumes for nutritional support, providing essential nutrients like carbohydrates, fats, proteins, vitamins, and minerals to sustain life, energy, growth, and repair. It is a fundamental necessity, serving as a source of fuel for the body and aiding in maintaining health and fighting disease. Human food, typically of plant, animal, or fungal origin, comes in diverse tastes, textures, and nutritional profiles, with a balanced diet of varied foods crucial for overall well-being.
